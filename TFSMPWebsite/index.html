Welcome to the TFSMP homepage!

TFSMP (Turboprop FS Multiplayer) is a mod for Turboprop Flight Simulator that allows you to play Turboprop FS together with friends over the Internet! You can either host a server for friends or the public, or join someone else's server. The possibilities are endless: you can challenge other players to a race, play hide-and-seek, join formation flights, go skydiving, or fly on someone else's plane!
